Welcome to Legend of Legends Base Stats program!

This program has a table of all the base statistics of all the champions within League of Legends

The data for this table is taken from the web each time this program is open, ensuring it is up to date

To get started click on either the Champion Stats button for their base statistics or the Champion Comparison button to compare

Key

HP: Health Points

HP+: Health Points gained per level

HP5: Health Regeneration

HP5+: Health Regeneration gained per level

MP: Mana Points

MP+: Mana Points gained per level

MP5: Mana Regeneration

MP5+: Mana Regeneration gained per level

AD: Attack Damage

AD+: Attack Damage gained per level

AS: Attack Speed

AS+: Attack Speed gained per level

AR: Armour

AR+: Armour gained per level

MR: Magic Resist

MR+: "Magic Resist gained per level
